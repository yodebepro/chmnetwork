EmailJS Direct Send Setup

One-time setup so forms send directly to your inbox without opening any email app. Free up to 200 emails/month.

Step 1 — Create free account:
Go to emailjs.com → Sign Up Free
Use your email: yodebepro@gmail.com
Step 2 — Add Email Service:
Dashboard → Email Services → Add New Service
Choose Gmail → Connect your Gmail account → Copy the Service ID
Step 3 — Create Email Template:
Email Templates → Create New Template
Set To email: yodebepro@gmail.com
Template content (copy exactly):
From: {{from_name}} ({{from_email}})
Phone: {{phone}}
Subject: {{subject}}

{{message}} Save → Copy the Template ID
Step 4 — Get Public Key:
Account → API Keys → Copy your Public Key
Step 5 — Paste all three below and click Save:
From Dashboard → Email Services
From Dashboard → Email Templates
From Account → API Keys

After saving, all contact forms, prayer requests, and connect buttons
on your website will send directly to your inbox instantly.
